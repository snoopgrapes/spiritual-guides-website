🛡️ 7-Day Spiritual Battle Plan for Zed

Tear down the root of bitterness and sexual sin. Rebuild with Christ.

🌅 Daily Rhythm (Morning + Night)

These are the two anchor points. Even if Zed fails during the day, he recalibrates at night.

Morning (15–30 minutes)

  • Worship (1–2 songs): Example – “Refiner” or “Gratitude” by Maverick City
  • Read one passage from the Daily Focus below
  • Pray the Bitterness Uprooting Prayer
  • Speak aloud: “I forgive my wife. I forgive myself. I walk in freedom today.”
  • Declare: “I am not a slave to sin. I am free in Christ.” (Romans 6:14)

Night (10–15 minutes)

  • Confess failures (1 John 1:9)
  • Journal: “What pulled me toward bitterness or lust today?”
  • Pray: “Lord, cleanse me tonight. I receive Your grace and strength for tomorrow.”

📅 7-Day Focus Topics + Scriptures

🗓 Day 1: The Root

Theme: Bitterness must be pulled out by the root.

Scripture: Hebrews 12:14–15

Focus: Ask God to expose hidden bitterness — not just what you're aware of.

🗓 Day 2: Forgiveness Is Warfare

Theme: Forgiveness is not weakness—it’s spiritual violence.

Scripture: Matthew 18:21–35

Focus: Write down specific offenses and forgive them aloud — even through tears.

🗓 Day 3: Sexual Sin Is a Symptom

Theme: Lust is the fruit, not the root. Bitterness often fuels it.

Scriptures: James 1:14–15, 1 Thessalonians 4:3–8

Focus: Ask: “What am I really seeking when I turn to porn?”

🗓 Day 4: Identity Detox

Theme: Your old self is dead. You are not your past or your temptations.

Scripture: Romans 6:6–14

Focus: Say aloud: “I am a new creation. I have power to say no.”

🗓 Day 5: Renounce + Replace

Theme: You must renounce sin and replace it with truth.

Scripture: Ephesians 4:22–24

Focus: Say: “I renounce bitterness, lust, and every lie. I receive truth, peace, and purity.”

🗓 Day 6: Freedom Is a Fight

Theme: Holiness isn’t perfection — it’s war.

Scripture: Galatians 5:16–25

Focus: Write: “What does walking in the Spirit look like for me?”

🗓 Day 7: Rebuild the Altar

Theme: Bitterness and sin break down the altar. Rebuild it in worship.

Scripture: Romans 12:1–2

Focus: Rededicate your body and story to God. Say: “I am Yours.”

💥 Bonus: Weapons for the Week

  • Fasting: Pick one day to skip lunch and pray.
  • Accountability partner: Even a text check-in helps.
  • Block porn access: Use filters/accountability tools.
  • Daily declarations: “I am not bitter. I am free. I am forgiven.”

🧱 Build Toward Week 2

  • Clarity about real roots of sin
  • Begin healing and forgiveness
  • Traction in spiritual warfare
  • Feel God's presence returning
