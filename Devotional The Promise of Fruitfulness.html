What fruit is Christ producing in my life?

Jesus promises that abiding leads to "much fruit" (John 15:5) - not just occasional good deeds, but a consistent Christlike character. The Greek word for fruit (karpos) implies visible, tangible results of spiritual life.

Reflection: Which fruit feels most evident in your life right now? Which feels most scarce? Remember - fruit grows naturally from abiding, not through self-effort.

‚úã Practice

Identify one fruit of the Spirit to intentionally cultivate today.

How to practice:

  1. Choose one fruit from Galatians 5:22-23 that you want to see more evident in your life
  2. Create a simple reminder (note on your phone, bracelet, sticky note)
  3. Look for 3 opportunities to express this fruit today
  4. At day's end, journal how Christ produced this fruit through you

Example: If choosing kindness, you might: (1) Let someone go ahead in line, (2) Send an encouraging text, (3) Listen attentively to a colleague.
