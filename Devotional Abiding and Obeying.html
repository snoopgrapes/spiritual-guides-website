üîç Focus

What commands of Christ do I find difficult to obey?

Jesus reveals the beautiful cycle of abiding and obeying: We obey because we abide, and our abiding deepens through obedience. Common challenging commands include:

  • Love enemies (Matthew 5:44) - Bless those who oppose you
  • Take up your cross (Luke 9:23) - Daily surrender
  • Forgive 70x7 (Matthew 18:22) - Unlimited mercy
  • Seek first God's kingdom (Matthew 6:33) - Reordered priorities
  • Make disciples (Matthew 28:19) - Intentional multiplication

Key insight: Jesus mentions joy (v.11) between His commands about obedience and love - showing that surrendered obedience brings delight, not drudgery.

‚úã Practice

Obey one prompting from the Spirit today.

How to cultivate responsive obedience:

  1. Prepare: Pray "Holy Spirit, make me sensitive to Your voice today"
  2. Pause when you sense a nudge (to pray, serve, speak, or refrain)
  3. Prompt: Ask "Is this from You, Lord?" (Test against Scripture)
  4. Perform the action within 24 hours (delayed obedience is disobedience)
  5. Process: Journal what happened and how it affected your abiding

Examples of promptings:

  • Call someone who comes to mind
  • Apologize to a person you wronged
  • Give anonymously to a need
  • Fast from something that distracts you from God
