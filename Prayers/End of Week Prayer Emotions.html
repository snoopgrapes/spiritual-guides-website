Heavenly Father,

As I come to the end of this week of emotional renewal, I thank You for walking with me through each day's lessons. You have shown me Your truth that sets me free from destructive emotions.

For the anger that flares within me, thank You for the wisdom of James 1:19-20 and Ephesians 4:26-27, reminding me that human anger doesn't produce Your righteousness. Help me to pause and consider Your perspective before reacting.

For the depression that sometimes overwhelms me, I cling to Your promise in Psalm 34:18 that You are near to the brokenhearted. Like David in Psalm 42:5, I will hope in You even when my soul is cast down.

For the fear and anxiety that plague me, I claim 2 Timothy 1:7 - You have given me a spirit of power, love, and self-control. As Philippians 4:6-7 instructs, I bring every worry to You in prayer, receiving Your peace that guards my heart.

For the frustrations of daily life, Proverbs 14:29 teaches me that patience comes from wisdom. Help me remember Ecclesiastes 7:8 - that the end of a matter is better than its beginning, and to trust Your perfect timing.

For the hurts I've carried, I receive Your healing promised in Psalm 147:3. As 1 Peter 5:7 says, I cast all my anxieties on You because You care for me. Mend my broken places with Your love.

For the joy I long for, John 15:11 reminds me that true joy comes from abiding in You. Let the joy of the Lord be my strength (Nehemiah 8:10) as I serve others in Your name.

For the peace I need, Isaiah 26:3 promises perfect peace to those whose minds are stayed on You. Let the peace of Christ rule in my heart (Colossians 3:15) in every circumstance.

Lord, continue the work You've begun in me. Help me to take every thought captive to obey Christ (2 Corinthians 10:5), to be transformed by the renewing of my mind (Romans 12:2), and to walk in the Spirit's fruit (Galatians 5:22-23).

May my emotions no longer control me, but rather be submitted to Your truth. Teach me to number my days that I may gain a heart of wisdom (Psalm 90:12).

In Jesus' name, Amen.
