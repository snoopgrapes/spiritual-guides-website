Prayer for Overcoming Fear & Anxiety

Heavenly Father,

I come before You today, acknowledging that fear has no place in the life You've called me to live. Your Word says You have not given me a spirit of fear, but of power, love, and a sound mind (2 Timothy 1:7). Yet sometimes, anxiety feels overwhelming—like a storm raging in my heart.

Right now, I lay down every worry at Your feet:

The fears that keep me awake at night...

The doubts that whisper I'm not enough...

The uncertainties about the future...

I exchange these burdens for Your peace, just as You promise in Philippians 4:6–7. Guard my heart and mind with the stillness that only comes from You. Help me to:

Remember Your power when I feel weak,

Rest in Your love when I feel alone,

Claim Your truth when lies bombard me.

Silence the noise of fear, and let Your voice be the loudest in my life. Fill me with courage to face each day, knowing You go before me.

Thank You that I am never defenseless—Your Spirit fights for me. Today, I choose to walk in faith, not fear.

In Jesus' mighty name, Amen.

Optional Reflection

After praying, sit quietly for a moment. Imagine physically handing your fears to Jesus and receiving His peace in return. Write down any Scriptures or promises He brings to mind.
