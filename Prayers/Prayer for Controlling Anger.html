Prayer for Controlling Anger

A Prayer for Peace and Self-Control

Your Word calls me to be "quick to listen, slow to speak, and slow to become angry" (James 1:19). Yet so often, my emotions flare before my spirit can respond in wisdom. Forgive me for the times I've let anger control my words, my thoughts, and my actions.

Lord, when frustration rises in me, remind me that "human anger does not produce the righteousness You desire" (James 1:20). Help me to pause, to breathe, and to seek Your peace before reacting. Before the sun sets today, I release every bitter thought, every unresolved conflict, and every spark of resentment into Your hands. Replace them with Your grace, Your patience, and Your love.

Jesus, be my peace. When my flesh wants to react, help me to respond with self-control (Galatians 5:22-23). Let my words be life-giving (Proverbs 15:1), and my heart quick to forgive‚Äîjust as You've forgiven me.

I surrender my emotions to You now. Rule my spirit, Lord, and let Your peace guard my heart and mind (Philippians 4:7).
