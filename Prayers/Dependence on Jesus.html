Jesus, Help Me Understand My Complete Dependence on You

Lord Jesus, my Savior and Sustainer,

I come before You with a humble heart, acknowledging that without You, I can do nothing. "Apart from me, you can do nothing." (John 15:5)

Open my eyes to see my utter need for You in every moment—for breath, for wisdom, for strength, and for grace.

Like a branch depends on the vine, teach me to abide in You. "Remain in me, as I also remain in you." (John 15:4)

When I am tempted to rely on my own understanding, remind me to trust in You with all my heart. "Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on your own understanding." (Proverbs 3:5)

You are the Bread of Life—feed my hungry soul. "I am the bread of life. Whoever comes to me will never go hungry." (John 6:35)

You are the Living Water—quench my deepest thirst. "Whoever drinks the water I give them will never thirst." (John 4:14)

You are the Way, the Truth, and the Life—lead me always. "I am the way and the truth and the life." (John 14:6)

Help me to say with the apostle Paul: "I can do all this through him who gives me strength." (Philippians 4:13)

May Your grace be sufficient for me, for Your power is made perfect in my weakness. "My grace is sufficient for you, for my power is made perfect in weakness." (2 Corinthians 12:9)

I surrender all to You, Jesus. Keep me close, dependent, and desperate for You every day.

In Your holy and precious name, I pray,
Amen.
