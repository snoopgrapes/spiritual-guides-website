A Prayer of Forgiveness

Heavenly Father,

I come before you today with a humble heart. Your Son, Jesus, taught us a powerful lesson about forgiveness in the parable of the unforgiving servant. Just as you have forgiven me for my countless debts, help me to forgive those who have wronged me.

Lord, it is so easy to hold onto anger and resentment. My heart can become hardened, and I find it difficult to release the hurt. But your word reminds me that if I do not forgive my brother from my heart, you will not forgive me.

Give me the grace to release the anger and hurt I hold towards others. Help me to remember the immense debt you have canceled for me through the sacrifice of your Son. Fill my heart with your love so that I may be a channel of your forgiveness to others.

In the name of Jesus, who taught us to forgive without limit, I pray.

Amen.
