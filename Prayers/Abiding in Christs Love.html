Abiding in Christ's Love

Beloved Jesus,

You call me to remain in Your love—not as a distant admirer, but as one embraced by You. "As the Father has loved me, so have I loved you. Abide in my love." (John 15:9)

When I feel unworthy, remind me that Your love isn't earned—it's given freely. "God shows his love for us in that while we were still sinners, Christ died for us." (Romans 5:8)

Anchor me in this truth: Nothing can separate me from Your love. "Neither height nor depth, nor anything else in all creation, will be able to separate us from the love of God in Christ Jesus." (Romans 8:39)

Teach me to receive Your love like a child—without suspicion, without bargaining, without trying to repay what can only be gifted.

Let Your love flow through me to others. May I love as You love—patiently, sacrificially, without conditions. "A new commandment I give to you, that you love one another: just as I have loved you, you also are to love one another." (John 13:34)

When I'm tempted to wander, draw me back to the safety of Your embrace. Your love is my true home.

In the name of Jesus, the embodiment of perfect love,
Amen.
