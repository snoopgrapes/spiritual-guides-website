Prayer for Healing

Christ understands our pain

Lord Jesus,

I come before You with a heart that's hurting. You know the pain I carry - the wounds that others don't see, the grief I try to hide, the disappointments that weigh me down. Your Word says You heal the brokenhearted, and today I ask for Your healing touch.

Thank You that You're not a distant God, but a Savior who understands my pain. You experienced betrayal, rejection, and sorrow during Your time on earth. When I feel alone in my suffering, remind me that You fully understand what I'm going through.

Help me to cast all my anxiety on You, trusting that You care for me. Give me the courage to release my hurt into Your hands, believing that You can bring beauty from these ashes.

Where I struggle to forgive those who've hurt me, give me Your grace. Where I'm tempted to dwell in bitterness, pour out Your love. Where I feel broken beyond repair, show me Your power to restore.

May Your healing work in my heart make me more compassionate toward others who hurt. Let my own experience of Your comfort enable me to comfort those in any trouble with the comfort I myself have received from You.

In Your precious name, Amen.

"The Lord is close to the brokenhearted and saves those who are crushed in spirit." (Psalm 34:18)
