The Joy of Abiding

Lord Jesus, my Source of Joy,

You promise that when I abide in You, Your joy will fill me completely. "These things I have spoken to you, that my joy may be in you, and that your joy may be full." (John 15:11)

Forgive me for seeking happiness everywhere but in Your presence. Awaken my heart to the deep delight of simply being with You. "In your presence there is fullness of joy." (Psalm 16:11)

When life feels heavy, remind me that abiding joy isn’t circumstantial—it flows from our unbreakable connection. "You will rejoice, and no one will take your joy from you." (John 16:22)

Let my life bear the mark of this joy—not superficial smiles, but the settled gladness of one who is fully known and fully loved.

May Your joy in me become contagious, drawing others to the Vine where true satisfaction is found. "The fruit of the Spirit is... joy..." (Galatians 5:22)

Jesus, my heart rests in this truth: To abide in You is to abide in joy itself.

In the name of the One who is my exceeding joy,
Amen.
