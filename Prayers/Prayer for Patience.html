A Prayer for Patience and Trust in God's Timing

Heavenly Father,

Thank You for Your perfect wisdom and unfailing love. Your Word reminds me that "Whoever is patient has great understanding, but one who is quick-tempered displays folly" (Proverbs 14:29). Help me to walk in this wisdom, trusting that Your timing is always right.

When delays and frustrations come, remind me to pause and ask, "What are You teaching me in this waiting?"

Keep me from haste and worry, and instead, fill me with faith that You are working all things for my good. As Ecclesiastes 7:8 says, "The end of a matter is better than its beginning, and patience is better than pride." Let me find peace in Your process, knowing that Your plans are worth waiting for.

Lord, when my heart grows restless, anchor me in Your promises. Teach me to surrender my timeline to You, believing that what You have prepared is far greater than what I can see. May my patience be a reflection of my trust in You.

In Jesus' name, Amen.

"Wait for the Lord; be strong and take heart and wait for the Lord." (Psalm 27:14)
