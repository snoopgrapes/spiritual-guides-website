A Prayer for the Removal of Bitterness

Heavenly Father,

I come before You with a humble and honest heart. Thank you for the revelations in Your Word, which are a lamp to my feet and a light to my path. Thank you for the grace You have poured out, a grace so powerful that sin no longer has dominion over me.

Lord, Your Word calls me to "strive for peace with everyone, and for the holiness without which no one will see You." I confess today that I have not been striving for peace, especially with my wife. A "root of bitterness" has taken hold in my heart, and it is causing trouble and defiling my relationship with her and, by extension, my relationship with You.

I recognize that this bitterness is a poison, a manifestation of the sin that Your grace has conquered. I have been failing to fully grasp and live in the grace You have so freely given me in Christ. I ask for Your forgiveness for harboring this resentment and for allowing a spirit of bitterness to take root instead of a spirit of love and forgiveness.

I ask in Jesus' name that You would expose this root of bitterness in my heart. Pull it out, Lord, by the power of Your Holy Spirit. Replace it with a spirit of gentleness, kindness, and grace. Help me to see my wife not through the lens of my bitterness, but through the eyes of Your love and mercy.

Remind me, Father, that I am no longer under the law of condemnation, but under the boundless grace of Your Son, Jesus Christ. Because of His work, I am free. Empower me with this freedom to forgive as I have been forgiven. Give me the strength to let go of the hurt and resentment that has grown, and to actively pursue peace in my marriage.

I surrender this bitterness to You now. I choose to let it go and to receive Your healing grace. Help me to live out the truth of Your Word: that because I am under grace, sin and bitterness will not have dominion over me.

Thank you for hearing my prayer. I trust in Your power to heal and restore.

In Jesus' holy name,

Amen.
