Prayer for Overcoming Depression

Scripture References:
Psalm 34:18, Psalm 42:5, Isaiah 41:10, Matthew 11:28, 2 Corinthians 1:3-4

Heavenly Father,

You see me in this place of pain, where my heart feels heavy and my spirit is weary. Your Word says that You are near to the brokenhearted (Psalm 34:18), so I cling to Your promise now. When darkness feels overwhelming, remind me that You are closer than my next breath.

Lord, when my soul is downcast, help me speak truth to myself like the psalmist: "Why are you cast down, O my soul? Put your hope in God!" (Psalm 42:5). Silence the lies of despair with Your voice of truth. Lift my eyes to You, my Savior and my God.

You tell me, "Do not fear, for I am with you; do not be dismayed, for I am your God" (Isaiah 41:10). Hold me secure in Your strength when mine fails.

Jesus, You invite the weary to come to You for rest (Matthew 11:28). I come nowâ€”burdened, tired, and aching. Wrap me in Your peace that surpasses understanding.

Thank You for being the God of all comfort (2 Corinthians 1:3-4). Even when I can't feel You, help me trust that You are working. Renew my hope, one step at a time.

In Jesus' name, Amen.

Additional Scriptures for Meditation:
"The Lord your God is in your midst, a mighty one who will save; he will rejoice over you with gladness; he will quiet you by his love." (Zephaniah 3:17)

"Cast all your anxiety on him because he cares for you." (1 Peter 5:7)

May God's peace guard your heart and mind as you trust in Him (Philippians 4:7).
