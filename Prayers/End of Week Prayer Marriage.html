üíí Prayer for Our Marriage

"Therefore what God has joined together, let no one separate."
- Mark 10:9

Heavenly Father,

As we come to the end of this week of intentional focus on our marriage, we thank You for meeting us in this sacred journey. You've reminded us through Malachi 2:14-16 that marriage is a covenant before You, not merely a human contract.

We confess our pride that has divided us (James 4:6-10). Humble our hearts as You showed us through Philippians 2:3-8. Help us consider each other above ourselves.

We release all unforgiveness, as You commanded in Colossians 3:12-14 and Matthew 18:21-22. Heal the wounds we've caused each other.

Teach us to love as a daily choice, not just a feeling (1 Corinthians 13:4-8). May we lay down our lives for each other as Christ did (John 15:12-13).

Restore our unity in prayer (Matthew 18:19-20). May our home be filled with Your presence as we seek You together.

Rebuild trust between us through consistent faithfulness (Luke 16:10). Help us keep our promises, no matter how small.

We claim Your promise in Joel 2:25 to restore what's been broken. Nothing is impossible for You (Jeremiah 32:27).

Fill us with hope for our future together. May our marriage reflect Christ's love for the Church. Continue the good work You've begun in us this week.

In Jesus' redeeming name, Amen.
