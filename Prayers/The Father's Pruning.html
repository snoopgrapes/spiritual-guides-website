The Father's Pruning

Heavenly Gardener,

I come before You, trusting that even Your cuts are acts of love. "Every branch that bears fruit You prune, that it may bear more fruit." (John 15:2)

When Your shears remove what feels precious, help me remember: You take away to give more. "He disciplines us for our good, that we may share His holiness." (Hebrews 12:10)

Where the wound is fresh, pour Your healing balm. Where I'm tempted to resent the loss, plant deeper trust. "The Lord gave, and the Lord has taken away; blessed be the name of the Lord." (Job 1:21)

Shape me through this sacred suffering. Let every cut expose more of Christ in me. "Though now for a little while you may have to suffer grief in various trials, this is so the tested genuineness of your faith may be found to result in praise and glory at the revelation of Jesus Christ." (1 Peter 1:6-7)
